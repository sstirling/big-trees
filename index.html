    The score

    Both states follow the American Forests big-tree formula:

    circumference (in.) + height (ft.) + ¼ × crown average (ft.)

    The result is the Points value on each card. Higher means a bigger tree. A tree can be the biggest of its species in its state (a Champion) without ranking near the top overall — small species don't compete with sycamores.
